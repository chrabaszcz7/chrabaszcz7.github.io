History

YouTube was founded by three former PayPal employees: Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim. The first video was "Me at the zoo" in 2005. Google acquired YouTube in 2006 for $1.65 billion.

How does it work?

YouTube is the largest video-sharing platform in the world. Users can upload, watch, comment, and share videos on almost any topic.

Popularity and uses

YouTube has over 2.7 billion monthly active users. It is the second most visited website after Google. Billions of hours of video are watched every day.
