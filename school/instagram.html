History

Instagram was founded by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger as an app for sharing photos with filters. In 2012, Facebook bought Instagram for 1 billion dollars. Since then, the platform has evolved by adding Stories, Reels, IGTV, and shopping features.

How does it work?

Instagram is a visual platform focused on photos and short videos. It allows users to share their lives, passions, and creativity through posts, Stories, and Reels.

Popularity and uses

Instagram has over 2.4 billion active users. It is especially popular among Millennials and Gen Z. The platform dominates the influencer marketing space.
