History

TikTok was created by the Chinese company ByteDance. The app gained massive popularity thanks to its algorithm that perfectly matches content to user preferences. In 2018, ByteDance acquired Musical.ly and merged it with TikTok, starting its global expansion.

How does it work?

TikTok is a platform for creating and sharing short videos, from a few seconds up to 10 minutes. It focuses on creativity, music, dance, comedy, and education. The "For You Page" algorithm perfectly matches content to each user's taste.

üéØ

Main features

  • Videos from 15 seconds to 10 minutes
  • Advanced video editing tools
  • Extensive library of music and sound effects
  • AR filters and effects (augmented reality)
  • "For You Page" algorithm recommending content
  • Duets and collaborations with other creators
  • Live streaming

Popularity and uses

TikTok has over 1.6 billion monthly active users and is one of the fastest-growing social media platforms. It is especially popular among younger users (Gen Z and younger Millennials).

Main uses:

  • Influencer marketing and advertising
  • Education (EduTok)
  • Entertainment and humor
  • Promoting small businesses
  • Creating viral trends

Why people use it

Easy-to-use content creation tools
Chance to reach large audiences quickly via algorithm
Entertainment and endless content feed
Community of creative creators
Opportunity to earn as a content creator
