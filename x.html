History

Twitter was founded by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams as a microblogging platform. In 2022, Elon Musk acquired Twitter for $44 billion, and in 2023 renamed the platform to X.

How does it work?

X is a microblogging platform where users post short messages (tweets) up to 280 characters. It focuses on current events, news, and discussions.

Main features

  • Tweets (text posts)
  • Retweets and quoting
  • Trends and hashtags
  • X Spaces (audio chats)
  • X Premium (formerly Twitter Blue)
  • Direct Messages
  • Community Notes

Popularity and uses

X has about 550 million active users. The platform is especially popular among journalists, politicians, celebrities, and people interested in current events.

Main uses:

  • News and breaking updates
  • Political and social discussions
  • Customer service for companies
  • Professional networking
  • Humor and memes

Why people use it

Real-time access to news
Participation in global conversations
Expressing personal opinions
Direct contact with celebrities and influencers
Quick updates on trending topics
